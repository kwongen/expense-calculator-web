Some of Steven Spielberg’s Movies

Title Year Stars Plot
Title: Firelight Year: 1964 Stars: Robert Robyn, Beth Weber, Lucky Lohr, Margaret Peyou Plot: Menacing flying saucers attack the citizens of a town
Title: Duel Year: 1971 Stars: Dennis Weaver, Jacqueline Scott, Eddie Firestone, Lou Frizzell Plot: A business commuter is pursued and terrorized by the malevolent driver of a massive tractor-trailer.
Title: The Sugarland Express Year: 1974 Stars: Goldie Hawn, Ben Johnson, Michael Sacks, William Atherton Plot: A woman attempts to reunite her family by helping her husband escape prison and together kidnapping their son. But things don't go as planned when they are forced to take a police hostage on the road.
Title: Jaws Year:1975 Stars: Roy Scheider, Robert Shaw, Richard Dreyfuss, Lorraine Gary Plot: When a killer shark unleashes chaos on a beach community off Cape Cod, it's up to a local sheriff, a marine biologist, and an old seafarer to hunt the beast down.
Title: Close Encounters of the Third Kind Year: 1977 Stars: Richard Dreyfuss, François Truffaut, Teri Garr, Melinda Dillon Plot: Roy Neary, an Indiana electric lineman, finds his quiet and ordinary daily life turned upside down after a close encounter with a UFO, spurring him to an obsessed cross-country quest for answers as a momentous event approaches.
Title: 1941 Year: 1979 Stars: John Belushi, Dan Aykroyd, Treat Williams, Nancy Allen Plot: Hysterical Californians prepare for a Japanese invasion in the days after Pearl Harbor.
